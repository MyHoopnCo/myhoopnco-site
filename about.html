About us

Our Story

Alberta has hoopers. Always has. But talent without exposure, resources, or community only goes so far.

Somewhere in Alberta right now, there's a hooper putting in real work. We're talking about early mornings, late nights, gym after gym. They've got everything it takes. What they don't have is a way to show it. No connections. No platform. No one to bridge the gap between their grind and the people who need to see it. The city kids get that bridge handed to them. Everyone else figures it out alone or doesn't figure it out at all.

No network to tap into. No way to show a coach, a scout, or a league what you're capable of.

MyHoop, Inc. is built to change that! For every hooper in Alberta, no matter where they're from or what resources they have.

We aim to be that platform this province always needed and never really had. And we're just getting started, with 120+ active members already in our community, keeping Alberta basketball connected every single day.
